AeroHelicon & Aviation Pvt Ltd was started in 2017 at Bangalore, India by aerospace experts with the objective of connecting Indian Aerospace Industry to the global aerospace. It started its operations with aircraft documentation, consulting & sourcing for aerospace companies. Today, it aiming to grow into a niche service provider and consulting organization for aerospace industry covering the entire aviation/aerospace life cycle: Design, Manufacturing & Aviation Services.

Overall, change is in the air! The signs from the government are refreshingly positive: There is a palpable change in mindset in the government in general to Aerospace Industries.
